Queeny Park Equestrian Events
Queeny Park Equestrian Events (QPEE) is a 501c3 not-for profit with a purpose of promoting and fostering the advancement of and interest in equestrian events, sports, training, teaching, competition of horses and riders, in the St. Louis area.

It started in 1978 when Wayne C. Kennedy, a former Director of St. Louis County Parks and Recteation, built a cross-country course at Queeny Park. He felt that the Park had the rolling terrain necessary for a good event, after visiting the 1978 World Championships at the Kentucky Horse Park. Mr Kennedy then asked Richard A. Wessel, then owner of Wessel Stables, to help oragnize this event, establishing QPEE.

The original cross country course was designed by Richard Newton who owrked to biuld it with Mr. Kennedy and Brock Fitzgerald, formerly of Queeny Park. In 1980-1981 Mr. Fitzgerald and Mr. Wessel became co-organizers of the Queeny Park Horse Trials, QPEE's signature event.
